Tumeric rice served with Beef stew

Ingredients (one serve)

  • 1 glass rice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 3 chopped onions
  • 1 \4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 sliced tomatoes
  • 1 \4 chopped beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 royco cubes
  • 1 spoon cornflour

STEPS:

  • Heat pan, add oil and saute onions.
  • Add the washed rice to the pan and stir, add turmeric powder and a pinch of salt and stir.
  • Add 2 1/2 glasses of hot water and let cook for 15 mins
  • Meamwhile, heat pan and brown the beef.
  • Add the remainig chopped tomatoes to the pan and stir.
  • Add royco cubes, a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  • Add the one spoon cornflour to the mixture for thickening and stir then add some water.
  • Let Cook for 25 mins.
  • Portion and serve. Remember to garnish
